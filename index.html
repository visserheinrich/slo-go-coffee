Our Happy Place

Slo-Go Coffee Co is your neighbourhood coffee shop in Brackenfell. We celebrate all the simple, yet best things in life - made by hand from scratch everyday.

We serve artisan coffee and freshly baked goods. Our biggest joy is to bring friends, family and the whole community together with ❤️ in the Cape.

In a world that constantly rushes forward, we invite you to take a step back, to breathe, to savour, and to embrace the unhurried life.

Our Story

You wake each morning feeling the weight of an endless to-do list — work pressure, family demands, a restless mind that never stops scrolling. You're aimlessly drifting, wondering if there's any room left for peace, purpose, or even a simple moment of joy.

Then you arrive at Slo-Go Coffee — a farm-style shop that invites you to join the unhurried life. The aroma of freshly brewed coffee and soft amber lighting greet you. On every coffee pouch you'll read: "A coffee that connects — this isn't just coffee, it's a movement." One sip slows the rush, grounds you, and opens a space where you can breathe.

"You never know how you can change someone's life."

I know that feeling, because I lived it. Years of overtime, anxiety, and a deep-down sense of hopelessness landed me in a dark hole — sleepless nights, a heavy heart, and a marriage that felt broken.

A barista once handed me a cup and said those words. His simple kindness, the support of my church, and the truth of Matthew 11:28–30 — "Come to me, all who are weary…" — showed me that surrendering control and trusting God could pull me out. That rescue became my mission: to create a place where anyone can find that same lifeline.

Now, when you come to Slo-Go Coffee, you're not just buying coffee — you're stepping into a community that uplifts, motivates, and reminds you that God is in control. You leave feeling lighter, more purposeful, and ready to face the day with a renewed sense of freedom. You become the hero of your own story, armed with a cup of intention and a network of people who cheer you on.

If the hurry of life is stealing your joy, come to Slo-Go Coffee. Grab a cup, join a conversation circle, and remember — we weren't meant to do life alone. Community needs us, and we need community.

— Chris, Slo-Go Coffee Co
