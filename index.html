Our Happy Place

Slo-Go Coffee Co is your neighbourhood coffee shop in Brackenfell. We celebrate all the simple, yet best things in life - made by hand from scratch everyday.

We serve artisan coffee and freshly baked goods. Our biggest joy is to bring friends, family and the whole community together with ❤️ in the Cape.

In a world that constantly rushes forward, we invite you to take a step back, to breathe, to savour, and to embrace the unhurried life.
